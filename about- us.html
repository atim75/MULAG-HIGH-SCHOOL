"To be a leading institution in providing quality primary education that nurtures lifelong learners and contributes to the development of the Mulago community and Uganda as a whole."

"To provide equitable, holistic education that equips pupils with knowledge, skills, and values to thrive in a changing world, while fostering discipline, creativity, and community engagement."

  1. Improving Academic Performance: Ensure pupils achieve proficiency in literacy, numeracy, and life skills through effective teaching and learning.
  2. Promoting Inclusivity: Provide equal opportunities for all children, including those with special needs, in line with UPE principles.
  3. Enhancing Infrastructure: Maintain and improve facilities (e.g., classrooms, water access, sanitation) to create a conducive learning environment.
  4. Community Involvement: Engage parents and the Mulago community to support education and child development.
  5. Moral and Civic Development: Instill values such as integrity, respect, and responsibility to prepare pupils for active citizenship.
