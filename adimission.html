ADMISSION FORM

At Mulago primary school, we’re dedicated to nurturing young minds and building a strong foundation for lifelong learning. Our primary school offers a warm, engaging environment where every child can grow, explore, and thrive. We’re excited to welcome new families into our community!

  • Birth Certificate or Passport: Proof of your child’s age and identity (PDF, JPG, or PNG).
  • Proof of Address: A utility bill, lease, or other document verifying your residence (optional for some schools).
  • Health Records: Up-to-date immunization records as required by [state/country] law.
  • Previous School Records: Report cards or teacher comments from prior schools (optional for Kindergarten applicants)
